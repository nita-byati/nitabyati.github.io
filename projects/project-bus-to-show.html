Bus to Show Informational Posters

Informational poster series designed to guide and inform audiences

Information Design Poster Series

Project Overview

The Bus to Show informational poster series was designed to provide clear, accessible information to audiences in a transportation context. These posters combine functional information design with engaging visual elements to ensure effective communication.

Design Challenge

Creating informational materials that work effectively in a transit environment requires balancing clarity, readability, and visual appeal. The posters needed to communicate important information quickly to people in motion.

Design Solution

I developed a cohesive visual system that prioritizes information hierarchy and legibility. The design uses bold typography, clear iconography, and strategic color coding to organize information and guide the viewer's attention.

Key Design Elements

• High-contrast typography for readability
• Intuitive iconography system
• Strategic use of color for organization
• Scalable design for various formats
• Accessible design principles

Implementation

The poster series was designed to work across multiple touchpoints, from large-format displays to smaller informational materials. The flexible design system ensures consistency while allowing for adaptation to different contexts and information needs.
