Font - "Eyce"

Custom typeface design exploring unique letterforms and character

Typography Font Design

Project Overview

"Eyce" is a custom typeface that explores the balance between legibility and artistic expression. The font was designed to have a distinctive character while maintaining readability across various applications and sizes.

Design Philosophy

The typeface design process began with extensive research into letterform construction and contemporary typography trends. The goal was to create a font that feels both modern and timeless, with unique character details that set it apart from existing typefaces.

Character Development

Each letterform was carefully crafted to ensure consistency in weight, spacing, and overall character. Special attention was paid to the relationship between letters, creating harmonious word shapes and optimal readability in both display and text applications.

Technical Specifications

• Complete uppercase and lowercase character sets
• Numerals and essential punctuation
• Optimized spacing and kerning
• Multiple weight variations considered
• Scalable vector format for various applications

Applications

The "Eyce" typeface is designed for versatility, suitable for branding applications, editorial design, and digital interfaces. Its distinctive character makes it particularly effective for headlines and display purposes while maintaining readability at smaller sizes.
